5-MIN CHOCOLATE MOUSSE

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • ¼ cup Baileys® Irish Cream (or equivalent)
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ cup powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Combine heavy whipping cream, Bailey's, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  2. Serve immediately or wait until cool.

And you're done !
